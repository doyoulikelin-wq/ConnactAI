What you get

A focused workflow for cold outreach: shortlist with evidence → one clean first email.

Find people with evidence

Each candidate includes match score, reasoning, and sources. Uncertainty is labeled when data is thin.

Generate a structured email

Why them → value exchange → one clear ask → opt‑out. Personalization comes from evidence, not fluff.

Reusable profile

We persist your sender profile + preferences so you don’t repeat input every time you log in.

How it works

Stay evidence-first. Keep the ask clear. Iterate fast.

1

Describe who you want to reach

Role + keywords + constraints. Add documents or links when you have them.

2

Review candidates with sources

Pick targets based on match score, reasoning, and evidence. Flag uncertainty instead of guessing.

3

Generate the first email

Subject → why them (evidence) → value exchange → one clear ask → opt‑out.

Example

Clear structure. Verifiable details. No invented connections.

Recommendation output

match_score: 0.86
match_reason: "Research overlaps in X; your project Y matches their recent work."
evidence:
  - https://example.com/paper
  - https://example.com/lab
uncertainty: "Title may be outdated; verify current role."

Email output

Subject: Quick question about <topic>

Hi <Name> — I’m reaching out because <why them (evidence)>.

In short, I’m <who you are> and I’m working on <what you’re doing>. I think I could help by <value>.

Would you be open to a 15‑minute chat next week? If easier, I can send 2–3 times and work around your schedule.

If now isn’t a good time, no worries at all — thanks for reading.

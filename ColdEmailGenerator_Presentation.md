## Design Notes for PowerPoint Creation

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: #16A085 (Teal)
- Accent: #FF6B9D (Coral)
- Background: #FFFFFF (White)
- Surface: #F8F9FA (Light Gray)
- Text: #2C3E50 (Dark Blue-Gray)

**Typography:**
- Titles: 48-64pt, Bold, Arial
- Body: 16-24pt, Regular, Arial
- Footer: 14pt, Regular, Arial

**Layout Guidelines:**
- Slide 1: Teal gradient background, centered white text
- Slide 2: Left coral accent bar (12px width)
- Slide 3: Two-column layout (35/65 split), left column teal background
- Slide 4: Horizontal flow with arrows, teal boxes
- Slide 5: Three equal-width columns with icon circles
- Slide 6: Left teal accent bar, two-column bullet lists
- Slide 7: Coral gradient background, white info box

All HTML slide files are available in /sessions/relaxed-vigilant-ptolemy/slide[1-7].html
